How To Best Print The Calendar

There are a few settings you can change in the print preview dialog to best present printed schedules:

1. Play around with the scaling

Depending on your operating system, your print preview may look different. Find the scaling dropdown and find the best text size.

2. Set the margins & Turn off headers and footers

3. Turn on 'Cram Rows' if there are many employees

In the case where you have many employees for every day and want to fit the calendar on one printed page, cram rows decreases the line space between the schedule text.
