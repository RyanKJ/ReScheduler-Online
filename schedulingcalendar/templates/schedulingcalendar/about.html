About Us

We are a Minnesota based startup building business driven scheduling applications.

Currently we are doing a beta test with A. Johnson & Sons Florist to build a different kind of scheduling app. One that is paradoxically anachronistic in that it caters to printed paper schedules, while being fully online ready. This is because many small retail businesses want features like a fully printable calandar filled with the information that they want, alongside a digital suite of features that makes scheduling easier such as cost projections.
